Welcome to Elephant Trail!

A perfect combination of modern luxury and untamed rural life, Elephant Trail is a three-star boutique hotel located on the border of the Udawalawe National Park. Setting high standards as a benchmark for lavish hospitality, Elephant Trail can be the safe haven for the weary yet excited travelers in southern Sri Lanka. The Elephant Trail maybe compact in size, but its sheer scale of hospitability along with endless activities to enjoy would be hard to ignore.

As the Udawalawe National park itself unravels from the front doors of Elephant Trail, a long stretch of sugarcane field surrounds from the rear. The southern rural life is abundantly visible to anyone enjoying the stay at the hotel. Open yourself to more than just a relaxed stay at the Elephant Trail as you can enjoy a range of activities from bird watching and bicycle rides to wild safaris in the famed Udawalawe National Park.
